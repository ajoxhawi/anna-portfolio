ABOUT ME

Hey there! I'm Anna my pen name, inspired by my middle name, Christiana. My full name is Anuoluwapo Christiana Olaopin, and I'm delighted to connect with you.

I'm a passionate creative writer - poet, novelist, scriptwriter, voice-over artist, and content creator. My journey began in 2021, when I discovered my deep love for storytelling. Since then, I've dedicated myself to crafting scripts, novels, and stories that inspire, provoke thought, and spark meaningful change. Transforming lives through words has always been at the heart of my mission.

I believe every voice matters. My work is driven by the conviction that someone, somewhere, needs to hear my message to feel empowered and make informed choices.

I've had the privilege of showcasing my talent through my short film "JUNE 5", written during my 2024 internship. As a professional voice-over artist, I deliver compelling and authentic performances that bring stories to life. I've also collaborated with respected clients such as The Bold Network and RCCG Blessed Arena.
